Cesar

Salad

  • 4
  • 15 minute
  • 78

Ingredients: 

  • 200 grams of Lettuce
  • 200 grams of Arugula
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 units of Egg
  • 30 grams of sliced bread
  • 30 grams of Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch of ground black pepper
  • 2 units of Anchovies (optional)
  • ⅓ cup olive oil
  • ½ lemon unit (juice)
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce or Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of mustard

Instructions

  1. wash and dry the lettuce and arugula leaves, and put them in a salad bowl, chopping them with your hands. Then cut the sliced bread into cubes to make the croutons or croutons.

  2. Crush the garlic clove in a mortar with a little salt and add 3 tablespoons of oil. Mix it a little bit, strain the oil and put a little more oil in a frying pan.

  3. Heat the oil and fry the cubes of sliced bread or croutons for a couple of minutes or until golden. Drain the excess oil on an absorbent kitchen paper and put them in the salad bowl on top of the lettuce and rocket leaves.

  4. Now mix all the ingredients of the homemade caesar sauce in this way: first mix all the ingredients, including the chopped anchovies if you want to put them, and lastly, emulsify everything with the olive oil.

  5. To finish, grate or cut the eggs into small pieces and put them in the salad bowl, then sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese on top and then dress the salad with the homemade Caesar sauce. And ready!
