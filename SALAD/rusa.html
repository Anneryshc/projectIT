Salad

  • 4
  • 55 minute
  • 105

Ingredients: 

  • Black olives
  • 1 6oz (170 grams) can of tuna
  • 2 cups of Mayonnaise, preferably home-made mayonnaise
  • 1 16oz (around 450 grams) can of peas and carrots
  • Roasted red peppers
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs
  • 6 medium-sized potatoes

Instructions

  1. Clean the potatoes to get rid of dirt and sand.

  2. Fill a large pot with water and bring it to boil. Put the potatoes unpeeled in the water and cook until they are cooked. Tip: Don't cook them too long as they will end up as mashed potatoes when you mix your salad.

  3. Once the potatoes are done cool them with cold water until you can handle them with your hands. Remove the peel of the potatoes and cut them into small cubes.

  4. Take a big bowl and add two cups of mayonnaise and one roasted pepper, sliced into strips.

  5. Use a fork to flake the tuna and add it to the bowl.Drain the water of the carrots and peas can and add them also to the bowl.

  6. Peel one of the hard-boiled eggs and add to the bowl as well.

  7. Now, mix all ingredients. If necessary, add more mayonnaise.

  8. To decorate your salad, add black olives, the second hard-boiled egg (cut in small pieces), and red pepper on top. and ready!
