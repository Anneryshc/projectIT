Green Salad

Salad

  • 4
  • 10 minute
  • 105

Ingredients: 

  • ½ Lettuce
  • 1 package of cherry tomatoes
  • ½ purple onion
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 1 can of black olives
  • 1 piece of Bell Pepper (red, green or yellow)
  • Feta cheese (optional)
  • 1 package of spinach sprouts
  • 1 splash of olive oil
  • 1 piece of Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. wash all the vegetables well

  2. a deep bowl or a salad bowl and start by chopping the lettuce and spinach sprouts with your hands. Cut these leaves into small pieces so that when serving and eating everything is much easier.

  3. Now cut the tomatoes in half and place them in the salad bowl.

  4. Finish chopping the remaining vegetables, the onion into thin rings, the julienned pepper and the unpeeled sliced cucumber. Add everything in the salad bowl.

  5. Also drain the black olives and cut the feta cheese into cubes. Make sure that the olives are pitted and if they are very large, cut them in half. Cheese is always an optional ingredient, but it will add life to the salad.

  6. To make the vinaigrette mix a little oil with vinegar, salt and pepper.

  7. Finally, add the vinaigrette to the salad bowl and mix everything together. Serve and enjoy this delicious green salad
