Pasta Salad

Salad

  • 6
  • 40 minute
  • 564

Ingredients: 

  • 300 grams of pasta spirals
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Walnuts
  • 80 grams of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 sprigs of basil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 150 milliliters of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta in salted water for the time indicated by the manufacturer. Drain it and refresh it with cold water. Drain again and drizzle with oil.

  2. Sauté the vegetables, Clean the pepper, wash it and cut it into sticks. Peel the onion and cut it into julienne strips. Peel the tomatoes, remove the seeds and cut them into cubes. Sauté the pepper and onion for 3 or 4 minutes in a pan with a few drops of oil.

  3. Peel and chop the nuts. To prevent the sauce from darkening, blanch the basil leaves before shredding.

  4. Make the basil sauce Reserve some basil leaves to decorate and crush the rest (previously scalded) with the peeled garlic, the Parmesan, half the walnuts, the remaining oil, salt and pepper, for 1 or 2 minutes.

  5. Mix the pasta with the sautéed vegetables, the tomato and some chopped walnuts. Water with the basil sauce and decorate with some of the reserved leaves.
