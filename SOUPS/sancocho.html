Sancocho











Preparation 1:30 hour
Total time 1:30 hour
Ingredients 10
Portion 8 person

Ingredient For Sancocho

  • 1 unit of chopped chicken
  • 6 units of pork chops
  • 220 grams of minced meat
  • 220 grams of chopped Yuca
  • 2 units of potatoes
  • 4 units of Banana
  • 1 unit of yam
  • 1 unit of Yautia
  • Â¼ Auyama unit
  • 2 units of Cob

Instructions

  • 1- 1 large green chili pepper finely chopped 1/2 sprig of parsley 2 small red onions, finely chopped 2 crushed garlic cloves 2 or 3 ancho cilantro leaves 2 chicken broth 1 beef broth 2 tablespoons of vinegar 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce In a large cauldron, place Put a little oil (2 1/2 tablespoons) and a little sugar (1/2 teaspoon) over high heat.
  • 2-Add the meat all together so that it softens a little and browns.Cover and leave until cooked (approx. 20-30 minutes), stirring from time to time, until much of the liquid is consumed.
  • 3-Remove the meat and set aside.In the same cauldron that the meat was boiled, add water (up to half the cauldron) with the rest of the liquid and let it boil; Pour the food and the vegetables for seasoning on top, cover, letting it boil for a long time (approx. 40 min.) or until the food is cooked.
  • 4- Then add the meat and the spoonful of Worcestershire sauce. Missing like 10 min. so that the sancocho is, add the vinegar (about 2 tablespoons).
  • 5-It is tested for salt and added if necessary. If plantain buns are desired, something very typical Dominican they are poured over them and allowed to boil until the sancocho is made. Plantain bun: Peel the raw plantain and guaya fine.
  • 6- Let cook a little and that's it
