Vegetables Soup







Preparation 30 minute
Total time 40 minute
Ingredients 8
Portion 4 person

Ingredient For Vegetables Soup

  • 4 cups low sodium vegetable broth
  • 1 large onion finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon finely crushed ginger
  • 1 pound of clean spinach
  • 2 bunches of kale,
    trimmed and trimmed
  • The juice of a fresh lemon
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil

Instructions

  • 1- In a large pot, heat the coconut oil over medium-high heat. Add the onion, garlic and ginger and fry for 10 minutes.
  • 2-Add the spinach and kale leaves. Season with salt, stir and cover the pot. When the leaves are soft add the broth. Cook over high heat until it boils and then reduce heat to medium. Cook for 25 minutes and turn off the heat.
  • 3-Blend the soup with a hand blender to obtain a fine puree. Taste it and adjust the salt if necessary. Drizzle with the lemon juice and serve immediately.
