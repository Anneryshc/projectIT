chicken Soup

Soups

  • 4
  • 1 hour
  • 75

Ingredients: 

  • 1 chicken carcass
  • 2 hen hindquarters
  • 1 leek
  • 3 carrots
  • 1 big onion
  • 4 parsley sprigs
  • Water
  • Salt
  • noodles for soup

Instructions

  1. Peel and clean all the vegetables. We do the same with the meat, discarding any remains of viscera that the chicken or hen may have. We put everything in a wide casserole and cover with water. Once it starts to boil, we can lower the heat and make the broth slowly, for approximately 1 hour.
  2. Remove the meat and vegetables from the pot and strain the broth. At this time we can refrigerate the broth until the next day, which will help us defat it better, or carefully remove the fat with the help of a spoon, if we want to make the soup immediately.
  3. Shred the vegetables and add a couple of tablespoons to the chicken broth. Thanks to the carrot, the chicken soup will have a beautiful hue. Heat the broth and when it starts to boil add the noodles and salt, lower the heat and leave until we like the pasta done. We serve immediately together with the shredded meat.
