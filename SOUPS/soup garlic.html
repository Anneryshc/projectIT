Garlic Soup

Soups

  • 4
  • 45 minute
  • 261

Ingredients: 

  • 1250 milliliters of water
  • 120 grams of stale bread
  • 1 head of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon smoked sweet paprika
  • 1 splash of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon olive or sunflower oil
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. We put water to heat in a pot. Meanwhile, peel and cut the garlic into thick slices.

  2. In a wide frying pan, add a drizzle of olive oil and when it is hot, saut√© the garlic. We remove from time to time until they are golden. We remove from the fire.

  3. Cut the bread into pieces and reserve. In the pan that we have just removed from the heat, add a generous teaspoon of smoked paprika, stir well and return to heat to continue with the traditional garlic soup recipe.

  4. Add the bread that we have broken and stir well so that it is impregnated with paprika, oil and garlic. When the water starts to boil, we pour the contents of the pan, stir well and add salt. Cover and let the garlic soup cook for 15 to 20 minutes.

  5. We beat the eggs as if we wanted to make an omelette and added them to the casserole, stirring. In less than a minute they will be curdled, so we turn off and serve.

  6. As we said, the traditional garlic soup is perfect to combat low temperatures, ideal for dinner. Bon Appetite!
