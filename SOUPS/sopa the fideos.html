Noodle Soup











Preparation 25 minute
Total time 1:07 hour
Ingredients 10
Portion 5 person

Ingredient For Noodle Soup

  • 3 liters of mineral or filtered water
  • 2 zucchini chopped
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 carrots cut
  • 2 chopped potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive or sunflower oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 sprig of thyme
  • pepper to taste
  • 2 garlic cloves

Instructions

  • 1- Peel and chop the carrots, garlic, onion and leek. In a pot, fry the garlic with oil for a few seconds until it evaporates.
  • 2- Add the carrot and onion and cook them for 5 minutes with a touch of salt. Lower the heat, add all the ingredients except the noodles.
  • 3- Next, pour the water. Stir and let the soup cook for approximately several minutes. After the time, add the noodles, beat again and wait for them to cook for 10 to 15 minutes.
