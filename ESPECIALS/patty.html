Pies

Especials

  • 10
  • 80 minute
  • 600

Ingredients: 

  • 2 onions
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 300 grams of canned tuna
  • 150 grams of fried tomato
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • olive oil
  • Salt
  • 500 grams of strength flour
  • 30 grams of fresh yeast
  • 300 milliliters of water
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the filling. Peel and chop the onion. Wash the peppers, remove the seeds and cut them into small pieces.

  2. Drain the tuna and crumble it with your hands.

  3. Pour a good jet of oil in a pan. add the onion, mix well and fry for 10 minutes making sure it doesn't burn. Add the peppers, more oil if needed, and cook for another 10 minutes.

  4. With a spoon, remove 10-12 tablespoons of oil from the sauce and reserve it for the dough, you can strain it so that it is clean of impurities.

  5. Add the tuna, tomato and paprika. Add salt to your liking, mix well and cook for another 5 minutes. Remove from heat, transfer the sauce to a bowl and let cool completely.

  6. time to Prepare the dough Dissolve the yeast in the water. If it is warm it will dissolve more easily.

  7. Put the flour in a large bowl and shape it into a volcano. Pour the water with the yeast into the hole of the volcano.

  8. Mix with your hands until the ingredients are integrated. Little by little add the oil from the sauce that you had reserved and knead until you get an elastic dough that does not stick to your fingers. If it costs, make rest so that the dough rests and is more manageable.

  9. Make a ball with the dough. Leave it inside a bowl greased with oil. Cover the bowl with a damp cloth and let the dough rise at room temperature for one hour. If it's cool, it's better to bring the dough closer to a radiator. On the contrary, if it's very hot, we can look for a cool corner of the house.

  10. After resting, the mass will have doubled its volume. Knead it lightly to remove the air. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 180ยบ with heat up and down.

  11. Cut the dough in half. Extend a part forming a square or rectangle with the help of a roller.

  12. Put the dough in the oven tray on a sheet of parchment paper. With a spoon, place the cold sauce on top of the dough, leaving the edges free to close the empanada later.

  13. Roll out the other dough in the same way, but save some dough to make a pair of decorative braids (optional).

  14. Cover the filling with the second dough and join it with the base dough by pressing the edges with your fingers. Shape some braids with the leftover dough and decorate the empanada to your liking.

  15. Prick the dough with a fork or make a chimney in the center of the dough. Brush the dough with the beaten egg to make it more golden.

  16. And bake 40 minutes at 180ยบ. If towards the end of baking you see that the top is getting too brown, cover it with parchment paper or aluminum foil. Let cool before serving.
