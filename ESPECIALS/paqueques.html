Pancakes

Especials

  • 2
  • 15 minute
  • 240

Ingredients: 

  • 1 cup self-rising flour or pastry flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 splash of olive oil
  • Â¾ cup of milk (180 milliliters)
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 1 splash of honey

Instructions

  1. gather all the ingredients. If you don't have self-rising or prepared flour, which is wheat flour that already includes yeast (also called pastry flour), you can use regular wheat flour and add a teaspoon of chemical baking powder (baking powder).

  2. Place the flour in a bowl and add the egg and milk. Mix well until these ingredients are integrated and there are almost no lumps.

  3. To finish making the dough for the homemade pancakes, add a splash of oil and mix well. You will see that the oil causes the flour lumps to dissolve and a smooth and homogeneous dough remains.

  4. To make the pancakes, heat a skillet over medium-low heat with a little oil or butter. When the pan is hot, add a tablespoon of the mixture and leave it on the heat until bubbles start to appear on the surface, then turn it over and cook on the other side.

  5. To serve the super easy pancake recipe, add a little butter and honey and enjoy.
