Tacos

Especials

  • 5
  • 30 minute
  • 226

Ingredients: 

  • 18 corn tortillas
  • 500 g of veal (beef)
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 tomatoes (tomatoes in Mexico)
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 2 serrano chiles (substitutable with jalape√±o, cayenne, habanero, etc.)
  • 2 poblano chiles roasted and cleaned (substitutable for roasted green peppers)
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Finely chop the onion, serrano peppers, tomatoes, garlic, and roasted chiles or peppers

  2. Place the oil in a skillet over medium heat with the onion. Once the onion begins to show through, add the shredded oregano and the minced garlic. Stir.

  3. Also add the tomatoes and serrano chiles; season to taste, stir and cook for about 10 minutes.

  4. Add the minced meat and integrate completely with the rest of the ingredients. Cook until the meat is done and the liquid is almost completely consumed.

  5. Then add the roasted peppers and add salt. Stir and cook for a few more minutes until the liquid in the mixture is completely consumed.

  6. Lightly grill the corn cakes on both sides and place a little filling in the center.

  7. Pass them from one side and the other through a pan with a little hot oil.

  8. Once fried on both sides, transfer the tacos to absorbent paper for a couple of minutes.

  9. Open the tacos slightly and add a little taco sauce, chopped tomato, lettuce, a little liquid cream and fresh cheese; to enjoy!
