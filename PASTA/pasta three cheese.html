pasta three cheese











Preparation 10 minute
Total time 25 minute
Ingredients 10
Portion 4 person

Ingredient For pasta three cheese

  • 8oz of linguine
  • ¾ cup of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 2 tbsp. finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 lemon, juice and zest (about 1
    teaspoon of the zest from the peel
    and 2 tablespoons of the juice)
  • ¼ tsp red pepper flakes
  • 2 tsp finely chopped fresh thyme
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced thin
  • Marinade with Pepper, to taste
  • 1lb large shrimp (16-20 pieces),
    peeled and deveined
  • ¾ cup of Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Instructions

  • 1- Cook linguine according to package directions. Drain the pasta and return to the pot. Set aside.
  • 2-Meanwhile, heat oil in a medium saucepan over low heat until hot (oil temperature should register 180°F on deep-fry thermometer). Season the shrimp with the Adobo. Add shrimp, garlic, thyme, and pepper; stirring so that the shrimp are coated with the oil. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the garlic is golden brown and the shrimp are pink and opaque, 8-10 minutes. Remove pot from heat. Add lemon juice and zest.
  • 3-Place the reserved pot with the linguine over low heat. Add shrimp mixture, stirring until pasta is coated with sauce and shrimp are evenly distributed, about 1 minute. Remove pot from heat. Add the parsley. serve.
