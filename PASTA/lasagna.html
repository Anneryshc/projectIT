Lasagna











Preparation 25 minute
Total time 50 minute
Ingredients 10
Portion 8 person

Ingredient For Lasagna

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 raw chicken sausage links,
    without casings
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • Pinch of red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup chopped fresh mozzarella
  • 1 (28 oz.) can crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups low-fat ricotta
  • 1/2 cup of 2% milk
  • 16 no-boil lasagna noodle sheets
  • 16â€“20 fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  • 1- Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the sausage and cook for about 3 minutes, until no longer pink. Add the onion, garlic, and red pepper flakes and continue cooking for about 5 minutes, until soft.the onion is soft and translucent.
  • 2-Add the tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes. then season with salt and pepper. y Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  • 3-Combine the ricotta and milk in a bowl. In a 9" x 9" baking dish, layer 4 noodles. Top with a quarter of the ricotta mixture and a quarter of the sausage mixture, then a few basil leaves and a quarter of the mozzarella.
  • 4- Repeat three times to create a four-layer lasagna. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, until cheese is melted and pasta is cooked through. Remove the foil and increase the temperature to 450 degrees Fahrenheit. Continue to bake for about 10 minutes, until the top of the lasagna is a nice golden brown.
