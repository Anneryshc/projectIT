Brownies











Preparation 10 minute
Total time 60 minute
Ingredients 9
Portion 12 person

Ingredient For Brownies

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa/ chocolate in powder
  • 1/2 cup monk fruit
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 125 grams of chocolate drops
  • vanilla
  • 1/2 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1/4 cup of walnuts (topping)

Instructions

  • 1- Start by preheating the oven. Then, beat the eggs at maximum speed until you get a frothy result. Add the monk fruit and the rest of the liquid foods.
  • 2- At low speed, add the dry foods and gradually place the mixture in a brownie mold or the one you have at home, previously greased with coconut oil.
  • 3- After that, add the nuts and bake for 45 minutes at 250Â°F.
  • 4- When the dessert is ready, wait for it to cool down a bit and divide into 12 portions.
