CAKE











Preparation 20 minute
Total time 1:00 hour
Ingredients 10
Portion 12 person

Ingredient For the cake

  • 1 banana
  • 1 Paraguayan
  • 1 baked apple
    (or 3 pieces of fruit to taste)
  • 65g gluten-free oat flakes
    or oat flour (or other flour)
  • 3 Eggs
  • cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon coconut or olive oil (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of yeast

the chocolate coating

  • 2-4 ounces of chocolate
  • 1/2 teaspoon of coconut
    or olive oil.

Instructions

  • 1- Peel the apple and cut it into small pieces, put it on a plate and heat it for 2 minutes in the microwave for 2 minutes (uncovered). Add it to a bowl along with the other chopped fruits and all the other ingredients, except the chocolate.
  • 2- Mix everything with a hand mixer and pour into the mold.
  • 3- Bake at 200ÂºC, for 20-30min (previously heated). Once ready, let cool before unmolding.
  • 4- Put the ounces of chocolate and coconut oil in a bowl. Microwave for 1 minute until liquid. Pour over the cake. Put in the fridge until the chocolate is hard, about 20 minutes.
