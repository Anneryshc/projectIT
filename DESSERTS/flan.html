Flan

Dessert

  • 10
  • 1:05 Hours
  • 560

Ingredients: 5 Egg, 1 (12 onz) Evaporated milk, 1 (14 onz) Condensed milk ,1 tablespoon vanilla ,1/2 cup of sugar.

Instructions

  • 1- Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)

  • 2- In a small heavy saucepan over medium-low heat, cook the sugar, stirring, until lightly browned. Pour into a round flan baking dish, tilting to coat bottom and sides. (already done this step we leave the saucepan in another place)

  • 3- In a blender we place the cup of sugar, the condensed milk, the evaporated milk, the eggs and the tablespoon of vanilla. and blend until a homogeneous liquid is obtained.

  • 4- Pour the liquid into the previous saucepan with the caramel, now we are going to make a bain-marie (it consists of placing a roasting pan and pouring a little water on it and placing the saucepan on top and taking it to the oven like this)

  • 5- Bake in preheated oven for 50 to 60 minutes, until center is set. Let cool for an hour on a rack, for better flavor chill in the refrigerator for 8 hours or overnight. To unmold, run a knife around the edges of the pan and invert onto a rimmed serving platter.
