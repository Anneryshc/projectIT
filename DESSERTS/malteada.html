Milkshake

Dessert

  • 2
  • 20 minute
  • 116

Ingredients: 

  • 6 tablespoon Ice cream (recommended vanilla with little sugar)
  • 2 cups milk of your choice
  • 3 tablespoon sugar
  • Whipped cream or chantilly and fruits (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a blender we pour all the ice cream, the milk, the sugar and stir

  2. optional step if you want it to be a different drink you can place fruits for more color and healthier

  3. When you get a homogeneous and slightly thick liquid, pour it into a glass and for a nice decoration place the cream and enjoy

    4. 8 hours or overnight. To unmold, run a knife around the edges of the pan and invert onto a rimmed serving platter.
