JELLY











Preparation 5 minutes
Total time 2:05 hours
Ingredients 3
Portion 4 person

Ingredient For the Jelly

  • 500 g of strawberry or fruit of preference
  • 1 envelope of unflavored gelatin.
  • Optional whipped cream.

Instructions

  • 1-Wash the fruits, and take 400g to the blender, and blend with a little water, reserve.
  • 2- In a pot, place 400 ml of water to boil, and when it is boiling add the flavorless gelatin, lower the heat and stir until dissolved.
  • 3- Once the gelatin is dissolved, add the strawberry or the fruit that you chose blended and stir very well. Remove from heat.
  • 4- Chop the remaining 100 g of fruit into pieces, and distribute them in the containers in which you are going to distribute the gelatin.
  • 5- Pour the gelatin mixture with strawberries into the containers, let it rest and put them in the fridge for at least 2 hours.
  • 6- When you take them out you can decorate with whipped cream and some fruits of your choice and eat.
